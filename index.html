About

I am an AI researcher focused on the intersection of Machine Learning and Computational Neuroscience. Having transitioned from a background in Nanotechnology Engineering, I recently completed my Master of Applied Science in Systems Design Engineering, where my research focused on world models and spatial semantic pointers.

My work aims to bridge the gap between high-dimensional neural representations and robust machine learning applications, specifically through the lens of Vector Symbolic Architectures (VSAs) and Geodesic Flow Matching. I am currently exploring applications for AI in material discovery and foundational research.
